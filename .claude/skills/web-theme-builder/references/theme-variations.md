## 1. Modern/Minimalist

### Description & Brand Personality
Clean, sophisticated, and uncluttered aesthetic that emphasizes content over decoration. Conveys professionalism, clarity, and forward-thinking values. Perfect for brands that want to appear modern, trustworthy, and user-focused.

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

**Light Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 13%)` - Near black
- Secondary: `hsl(0, 0%, 45%)` - Neutral gray
- Accent: `hsl(210, 100%, 50%)` - Pure blue
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 98%)` - Off-white
- Text Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 13%)`
- Text Secondary: `hsl(0, 0%, 45%)`

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 95%)` - Near white
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 10%)` - Deep charcoal
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 15%)` - Charcoal gray
- Accent: `hsl(210, 100%, 60%)` - Brighter blue

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
- Weights: 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Excellent for headings with modern geometric clarity

**Text Font:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Maintains consistency and readability

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit) for display
- [Work Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Work+Sans) for body

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Section padding: 96px - 128px (vertical)
- Component spacing: 24px - 48px
- Generous white space (60%+ of viewport)

**Border Radius:**
- Buttons: 4px - 8px
- Cards: 8px - 12px
- Minimal rounding for sharp aesthetic

**Grid Patterns:**
- 12-column grid
- Large gutters (32px - 48px)
- Asymmetric layouts with strong vertical rhythm

### Real-world Examples
- **Apple** (apple.com) - Ultimate minimalist aesthetic
- **Stripe** (stripe.com) - Clean, professional, modern
- **Figma** (figma.com) - Design-forward minimalism
- **Linear** (linear.app) - Contemporary product design

### Best Used For
- Tech startups and SaaS products
- Design agencies and creative studios
- Professional services (consulting, legal)
- Apps focused on user experience
- **Target Audience:** Tech-savvy professionals, designers, modern businesses

## 2. Corporate/Professional

### Description & Brand Personality
Traditional, trustworthy, and authoritative design that conveys stability and expertise. Emphasizes credibility, heritage, and enterprise-level professionalism. Ideal for established organizations and B2B services.

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

**Light Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(220, 90%, 25%)` - Deep navy blue
- Secondary: `hsl(210, 15%, 55%)` - Corporate gray-blue
- Accent: `hsl(200, 100%, 40%)` - Professional blue
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - Pure white
- Surface: `hsl(210, 20%, 96%)` - Cool light gray
- Text Primary: `hsl(220, 20%, 20%)`
- Text Secondary: `hsl(210, 10%, 45%)`

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(210, 100%, 65%)` - Lighter blue
- Background: `hsl(220, 18%, 12%)` - Navy black
- Surface: `hsl(220, 15%, 18%)` - Dark slate

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Montserrat](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat)
- Weights: 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Professional and authoritative

**Text Font:** [Open Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semibold)
- Highly readable for corporate content

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Roboto](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto) for universal appeal
- [Lato](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Lato) for friendly professionalism

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Section padding: 64px - 96px
- Conservative spacing with clear hierarchy
- Structured content blocks

**Border Radius:**
- Buttons: 4px
- Cards: 6px - 8px
- Minimal rounding for professional look

**Grid Patterns:**
- Traditional 12-column grid
- Symmetrical layouts
- Clear visual hierarchy with boxes and containers

### Real-world Examples
- **IBM** (ibm.com) - Enterprise corporate design
- **Salesforce** (salesforce.com) - B2B professional aesthetic
- **Deloitte** (deloitte.com) - Traditional consulting firm
- **Microsoft** (microsoft.com) - Corporate tech standard

### Best Used For
- Financial institutions and banks
- Consulting and professional services
- Enterprise software (B2B)
- Government and educational institutions
- Healthcare organizations
- **Target Audience:** Business executives, enterprise clients, institutional buyers

- Text Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 10%)`
- Text Secondary: `hsl(340, 10%, 35%)`

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(340, 100%, 65%)` - Bright magenta
- Background: `hsl(280, 30%, 10%)` - Deep purple-black
- Surface: `hsl(280, 25%, 15%)` - Purple charcoal
- Accent: `hsl(45, 100%, 60%)` - Bright yellow

- Weights: 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold)

- Modern and readable with personality

- [Manrope](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Manrope) for body text

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px

**Border Radius:**

- Cards: 16px - 24px
- Playful, rounded aesthetic

**Grid Patterns:**
- Broken/asymmetric grids
- Diagonal layouts
- Overlapping content sections
- Bold typography as layout element

### Real-world Examples
- **Spotify** (spotify.com) - Vibrant brand colors and bold design
- **Mailchimp** (mailchimp.com) - Playful and creative
- **Dropbox** (dropbox.com) - Colorful and engaging
- **Webflow** (webflow.com) - Design-forward and bold

### Best Used For
- Creative agencies and design studios
- Entertainment and media brands
- Music and event platforms
- Marketing and advertising firms
- Youth-focused products
- **Target Audience:** Creatives, young professionals, trendsetters, artists

## 4. Dark/Tech

### Description & Brand Personality
Sleek, futuristic, and sophisticated design with dark backgrounds. Conveys innovation, technical expertise, and premium quality. Popular in gaming, crypto, and cutting-edge tech sectors.

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

**Dark Mode (Primary):**
- Primary: `hsl(260, 100%, 65%)` - Electric purple
- Secondary: `hsl(180, 100%, 45%)` - Cyan
- Accent: `hsl(340, 100%, 60%)` - Hot pink
- Background: `hsl(240, 15%, 8%)` - Deep space black
- Surface: `hsl(240, 10%, 12%)` - Dark charcoal
- Text Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 95%)` - Near white
- Text Secondary: `hsl(240, 5%, 70%)` - Cool gray

**Light Mode (Alternative):**
- Background: `hsl(240, 20%, 96%)` - Cool light gray
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Inverted accent colors

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Space Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk)
- Weights: 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Futuristic and technical

**Text Font:** [IBM Plex Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Sans)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Technical yet readable

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Orbitron](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Orbitron) for ultra-tech feel
- [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono) for code-heavy sites

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Glowing borders and neon accents
- Generous padding: 80px - 120px
- Floating card elements

**Border Radius:**
- Buttons: 8px - 12px
- Cards: 12px - 16px
- Moderate rounding with glows

**Grid Patterns:**
- Grid overlays and technical lines
- Isometric or 3D elements
- Particle effects and animations
- Glass morphism effects

### Real-world Examples
- **Vercel** (vercel.com) - Dark dev platform aesthetic
- **GitHub** (github.com) - Technical dark interface
- **Binance** (binance.com) - Crypto/trading platform
- **NVIDIA** (nvidia.com) - Gaming/tech hardware

### Best Used For
- Developer tools and platforms
- Cryptocurrency and blockchain
- Gaming and esports
- Cybersecurity services
- AI and machine learning products
- **Target Audience:** Developers, gamers, tech enthusiasts, crypto traders

- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Text Primary: `hsl(25, 30%, 25%)` - Warm brown
- Text Secondary: `hsl(25, 15%, 45%)` - Soft brown

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(25, 85%, 65%)` - Bright orange
- Background: `hsl(25, 20%, 12%)` - Warm dark brown

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Nunito](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Nunito)

- Rounded and friendly

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Quicksand](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Quicksand) for extra friendliness
- [Karla](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Karla) for clean readability

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Comfortable spacing: 64px - 96px

- Curved dividers between sections

**Border Radius:**
- Buttons: 24px - 32px (rounded pills)
- Cards: 16px - 24px (soft corners)
- Generous rounding throughout

**Grid Patterns:**
- Flexible, card-based layouts
- Circular images and avatars
- Hand-drawn illustrations
- Organic shapes and blobs

### Real-world Examples
- **Slack** (slack.com) - Friendly workplace communication
- **Duolingo** (duolingo.com) - Approachable learning

### Best Used For
- Education and learning platforms
- Health and wellness services
- Community and social platforms
- Food and hospitality
- Non-profit organizations
- **Target Audience:** General consumers, families, community members, students

## 6. E-commerce/Product

### Description & Brand Personality
Conversion-focused design that highlights products and drives purchases. Emphasizes trust, clarity, and seamless shopping experience. Balances visual appeal with functionality.

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

**Light Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(215, 100%, 50%)` - Trust blue
- Secondary: `hsl(215, 15%, 30%)` - Slate gray
- Accent: `hsl(145, 65%, 45%)` - Success green
- Alert: `hsl(0, 85%, 55%)` - Error red
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 98%)` - Light gray
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Text Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 15%)`
- Text Secondary: `hsl(0, 0%, 45%)`

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(215, 100%, 60%)` - Lighter blue
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 10%)` - Dark charcoal
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 14%)` - Card gray

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Plus Jakarta Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Plus+Jakarta+Sans)
- Weights: 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- Modern and commercial

**Text Font:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Clear pricing and product details

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Sora](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Sora) for display
- [Rubik](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Rubik) for friendly commerce

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Product grid spacing: 16px - 24px
- Clear CTAs with prominent placement
- Sticky headers and cart icons

**Border Radius:**
- Buttons: 6px - 8px
- Product cards: 8px - 12px
- Input fields: 4px - 6px

**Grid Patterns:**
- Product grids: 2-4 columns (responsive)
- Card-based layouts
- Clear visual hierarchy for pricing
- Badge and label systems

### Real-world Examples
- **Shopify** (shopify.com) - E-commerce platform leader
- **Amazon** (amazon.com) - Conversion-optimized retail
- **Etsy** (etsy.com) - Marketplace aesthetic
- **Nike** (nike.com) - Premium product showcase

### Best Used For
- Online stores and marketplaces
- Product showcase websites
- Retail brands
- Subscription services
- Digital product sales
- **Target Audience:** Online shoppers, retail consumers, product buyers

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

- Warning: `hsl(40, 100%, 50%)` - Amber
- Error: `hsl(0, 85%, 55%)` - Red
- Background: `hsl(210, 20%, 98%)` - Cool white
- Surface: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Border: `hsl(210, 15%, 90%)` - Subtle gray

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(210, 100%, 60%)` - Lighter blue
- Background: `hsl(220, 15%, 10%)` - Dark slate
- Surface: `hsl(220, 12%, 14%)` - Card dark
- Border: `hsl(220, 10%, 20%)` - Subtle border

### Typography Recommendations

**Grid Patterns:**

## 8. Portfolio/Personal

### Description & Brand Personality
Expressive, unique, and personality-driven design that showcases individual work and creativity. Balances professionalism with personal flair. Designed to make a memorable impression.

### Color Palette (HSL Values)

**Light Mode (Customizable):**
- Primary: `hsl(280, 70%, 50%)` - Creative purple
- Secondary: `hsl(200, 80%, 45%)` - Sky blue
- Accent: `hsl(340, 85%, 55%)` - Vibrant pink
- Background: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - White
- Surface: `hsl(280, 30%, 98%)` - Subtle purple tint
- Text Primary: `hsl(0, 0%, 10%)`
- Text Secondary: `hsl(0, 0%, 40%)`

**Dark Mode:**
- Primary: `hsl(280, 80%, 65%)` - Bright purple
- Background: `hsl(280, 15%, 10%)` - Deep purple-black
- Surface: `hsl(280, 12%, 15%)` - Purple charcoal

### Typography Recommendations

**Display Font:** [Playfair Display](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Playfair+Display) or [Libre Baskerville](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Libre+Baskerville)
- Weights: 700 (Bold), 900 (Black)
- Elegant and distinctive

**Text Font:** [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) or [Lato](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Lato)
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semibold)
- Readable and professional

**Alternative Pairings:**
- [Fraunces](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fraunces) for unique personality
- [Cabinet Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cabinet+Grotesk) for modern feel

### Layout Characteristics

**Spacing Patterns:**
- Base unit: 8px
- Hero sections: 100vh or 80vh
- Generous project spacing: 96px - 160px
- Breathing room for visual work

**Border Radius:**
- Varies by style (0px for minimal, 16px+ for soft)
- Consistent within chosen aesthetic
- Image borders: 8px - 16px

**Grid Patterns:**
- Masonry layouts for project galleries
- Full-width hero sections
- Bento box grid layouts
- Asymmetric content blocks

### Real-world Examples
- **Bruno Simon** (bruno-simon.com) - Interactive 3D portfolio
- **Brittany Chiang** (brittanychiang.com) - Developer portfolio
- **Awwwards Winners** - Various creative portfolios
- **Behance Portfolios** - Designer showcases

### Best Used For
- Designer and developer portfolios
- Artist and photographer websites
- Freelancer showcase sites
- Personal branding
- Creative professional resumes
- **Target Audience:** Potential clients, recruiters, collaborators, peers
